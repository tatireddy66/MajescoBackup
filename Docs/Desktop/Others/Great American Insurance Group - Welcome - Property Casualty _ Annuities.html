The Strength of Specialization

Great American Insurance Group is committed to helping individuals and businesses secure a healthy financial future. We provide innovative insurance solutions and specialize in serving niche marketplaces that we know well. This expertise has given us the foundation for success that spans generations.

There’s strength in our numbers!

There are over 3,000 property and casualty insurance companies in the United States.
Only 50 are included on the Ward’s 50 List for safety, consistency and performance.
Only 5 have been rated “A” or better by A.M. Best for over 100 years.
Only 2 are on both lists.
Great American Insurance Company is 1 of the two.*
